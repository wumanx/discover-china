Essential Info for Japanese Travelers

✈️ Flights

Direct flights from Tokyo, Osaka, and Nagoya to Beijing, Shanghai, and Chengdu. Flight time: 3–4 hours.

🛂 Visa

Japanese citizens can enjoy visa-free entry for up to 15 days. Longer stays require a tourist visa — easy to obtain.

💱 Currency

Chinese Yuan (CNY). 1 JPY ≈ 0.048 CNY. Mobile payments (Alipay, WeChat Pay) are widely accepted.

🗣️ Language

Mandarin Chinese. English signage in major tourist areas. Translation apps work well for communication.
